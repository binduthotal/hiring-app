Corporate Readiness Program

Corporate Readiness Program

Learn & Navigate through the Complex Corporate World and know the right ways to build a great career.

Program Philosophy

The College environment is totally different from the Corporate World. For many its a Culture shock and for many it’s difficult to navigate through the Corporate World. This Program makes the transition smoother.

What is corporate readiness?

Corporate life and expectations are very different from student life. Today’s leading corporates demand their entry level talent to we well trained in many important areas such as Problem Solving, Professional Etiquette, and Team Work, amongst others. In addition they also expect that students know the unwritten rules of the corporate world before entering it. Most of the students are underprepared in these areas due to lack of appropriate training and guidance in their colleges. The challenge gets compounded as many students also don’t have access to relatives or friends working in corporate to help them build the relevant skills.

Why this program?

Skills imparted by faculty/placement cell in many colleges are short of what students need, to successfully transition from college to corporate environment.


Students are not able to demonstrate the strong initial impression despite having required skill for the job.


GVMS infotech bridges this gap by providing appropriate training through its professional faculty which has rich experience of working with leading Indian and Multinational corporations.


This program is designed to develop fundamental hard and soft skills in students to help seamlessly and successfully transition into a corporate career.

Participant's Profile:

Any Graduates who are getting ready for their final placements, or already landed a job and getting ready to join the Corporate World


Engineering or Management ; Pharmacy Graduates who will be taking up their first job.


Summer Interns who will be undergoing their 2-3 months of Summer Internship in selected organizations.

What is included in the course?

  • Transition – from college to corporate

  • First impressions, Professional etiquette and Business ethics

  • How to present one-self in groups

  • Business communication skills ( verbal & written )

  • Getting into a Solution Orientation mode

  • Importance of Team Work and understanding Group Dynamics>

  • The importance of developing a meaningful network, & seeking guidance

  • Individual SWOT analysis

  • Corporate structure and your role

  • Problem solving skills

  • Business IT skills ( MS outlook, MS excel, MS PowerPoint and MS word )

  • Other Important aspects of Building a Successful Career

What are the benefits for you?

Strong foundation of skills that will help you give distinct advantage over peers in placement & career growth
